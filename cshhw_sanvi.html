Introduction to Biodiversity

Click on this image to view an in depth explanation of the topic

Biodiversity refers to the variety of life on Earth at all its levels, from genes to ecosystems. This includes the evolutionary, ecological, and cultural processes that sustain life. Biodiversity boosts ecosystem productivity where each species, no matter how small, all have an important role to play.

Biodiversity is essential for the processes that support all life on Earth, including humans. Without a wide range of animals, plants and microorganisms, we cannot have the healthy ecosystems that we rely on to provide us with the air we breathe and the food we eat. And people also value nature of itself.
